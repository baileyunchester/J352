As the most decorated American major sports franchise, the New York Yankees have found themselves in a bit of a drought since their last World Series victory in 2009. Despite posing as consistently legitimate contenders for the past few years, with lineups consisting of generational players, the Yankees just can't seem to bring the trophy back to New York.
