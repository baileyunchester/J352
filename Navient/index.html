Navient Solutions Sees Increase in Complaints in Certain States




Navient Solutions complaints by state seemed to be heavily related to general state population and number of residents who attend collegiate institutions. The top five U.S. States for total population college enrollment are California, Texas, New York, Florida, and Illinois in that order, according to statsamerica.org. Four of those five states listed are included in the top states for Navient Solutions complaints from May 3, 2024 to May 3, 2025, per Student Loan Planner and CFPB

These complaints are primarily revolved around issues with misapplied payments, difficulties with loan services, and difficulties with income-driven payment plans. Despite recent lawsuits to Navient Solutions, the number of complaints climbs each year, especially in these high populated states with large numbers of collegiate enrollment. A major settlement was announced in January of 2022 according to Student Loan Planner, which required Navient to provide a total of $1.85 billion in relief to private and federal student loan borrowers. Despite this scrutiny, complaint levels are still rising annually.
